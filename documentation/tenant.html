    Tenant Panel

    Tenants Account Activities¶

    Tenants can access his information, create Tickets, Show Notice from Owners.

    Dashboard¶

    In Zaiporty, Tenants can see Property list, Tickets, Invoice lists and other information.

    Invoice¶

    Tenats can see all invoice created against him and also can print it.

    Tickets¶

    Tenats can see his previous tickets and also create new tickets

    Information¶

    Tenants can see infromations related to the properties which are added from the owner panel.

    Documents¶

    Tenants can upload , edit, delete infromation related to him.

    Profile¶

    My Profile¶

    Tenants can change infromation about him.

    Change Password¶

    Tenants also can change his password.
